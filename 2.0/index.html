Bio

import_contacts

         Travis James was born in Small Town, MT. He started his education pursuing acting and psychology at Montana State University before transferring to Portland State University. He is now known for his roles as The Rookie in Sundance Film Festival’s "The Orphan Girl,” as Brent in the feature "Twenty-Five Five,” and more! Travis has a very laid back and professional personality that makes collaborating and getting along with different types of people very peaceful and straightforward.


I try my best to treat acting the same way a professional athlete treats his craft. It requires professionalism, yes, but the best way to get ahead is to set specific and realistic goals for improving both your situation and your craft. Sometimes, it’s as simple as that. Aim high, stay focused, and just make it happen I guess.

-Travis James
