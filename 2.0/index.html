Bio

Travis James was born and raised in Kalispell, Montana. Through both grade school and high school he discovered his passion: ACTING.

After graduating Flathead High School, Travis began to put his dreams into action.

College

Travis James studied in Flathead Valley Community College before heading to Montana State University and eventually moved on to Oregon State University.

Roles

Travis James is now known for his roles as The Rookie in "The Orphan Girl," the story of a mining community in Billings Montana, and as Brent in "Twenty-Five Five," a tale of a mother whos luck turns when she stumbles upon a winning lottery ticket.
